About

I am a PhD student in the statistics department at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam under supervision of Frank van der Meulen and Moritz Schauer. My research focusses on statistical inference for stochastic processes. Here, I focus on performing inference using simulation of bridges for a wide class of Markov process such as chemical reaction processes, solutions to SDEs and process on Riemannian manifolds.
