About

I am a lecturer at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam (VU), where I mainly focus on teaching courses in statistics for various groups of students. I completed my PhD at the VU under supervision of Frank van der Meulen and Moritz Schauer. My research focusses on statistical inference for stochastic processes. Here, I focus on performing inference using simulation of bridges for a wide class of Markov process such as chemical reaction processes, solutions to SDEs and process on Riemannian manifolds. The first two years were carried out at the Technische Universiteit Delft.
